# Asset Inventory

## Overview

This document identifies key assets used by GoldLeaf Accounting LLC that support business operations.

Understanding these assets helps identify potential cybersecurity risks and prioritize protection measures.

## Asset List

### Employee Laptops

Employees use company laptops to access accounting software, email, and client data.

Importance:
High — these systems store sensitive financial data and access company resources.

---

### Home WiFi Networks

Employees connect to company resources through their personal home WiFi networks.

Importance:
High — insecure networks could allow attackers to intercept traffic or access systems.

---

### Email System

The company uses cloud email services (Google Workspace or Microsoft 365) for communication with clients.

Importance:
High — email is a common entry point for phishing attacks.

---

### Cloud File Storage

Client documents and financial records are stored in cloud storage systems.

Importance:
Critical — loss or compromise of this data could severely impact business operations.

---

### Accounting Software

The company relies on accounting software to manage financial data and client records.

Importance:
Critical — this system is essential for business operations.

---

### Employee Credentials

Employees use usernames and passwords to access systems.

Importance:
Critical — stolen credentials could allow attackers to access sensitive company data.
