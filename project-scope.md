# Project Scope

## Project Title

Cybersecurity Risk Assessment for a Small Business Environment

## Objective

The objective of this project is to perform a cybersecurity risk assessment for a simulated small business environment.
The assessment identifies assets, potential threats, vulnerabilities, and security risks that could affect business operations.

## Organization Being Assessed

GoldLeaf Accounting LLC (fictional company)

GoldLeaf Accounting is a small accounting firm with approximately 8 employees.
Employees work remotely from home offices and use cloud services to manage accounting operations and client data.

## Environment Description

The company relies on the following technologies:

* Employee laptops
* Home WiFi networks
* Cloud services (Google Workspace / Microsoft 365)
* Email communication
* Accounting software
* File storage in the cloud

Employees connect to company resources through the internet using their personal home networks.

## Assessment Goals

The assessment aims to:

* Identify key assets used by the company
* Identify possible cybersecurity threats
* Evaluate potential risks to company data and systems
* Recommend security improvements

## In Scope

The following items are included in this assessment:

* Employee laptops
* Home office WiFi networks
* Email systems
* Cloud file storage
* Employee authentication and access

## Out of Scope

The following items are not included in this assessment:

* Physical security of office buildings
* Third-party vendor internal systems
* Detailed penetration testing

## Methodology

This project follows general cybersecurity risk assessment concepts inspired by frameworks such as the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.
