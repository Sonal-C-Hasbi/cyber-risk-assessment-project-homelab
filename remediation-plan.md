# Cybersecurity Remediation Plan

## Overview

This document provides recommended security improvements for GoldLeaf Accounting LLC based on the identified cybersecurity risks.

The goal of this remediation plan is to reduce the likelihood and impact of cyber attacks by implementing appropriate security controls.

Recommendations are prioritized based on risk severity.

---

## Priority 1 — Immediate Risk Reduction

### Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Description:
Require multi-factor authentication for all employee accounts accessing email and cloud services.

Reason:
MFA significantly reduces the risk of account compromise caused by phishing or stolen credentials.

Affected Systems:

* Email accounts
* Cloud storage
* Accounting software

---

### Implement Endpoint Protection

Description:
Install endpoint protection or antivirus software on all employee laptops.

Reason:
Endpoint protection helps detect and prevent malware and ransomware infections.

Affected Systems:

* Employee laptops

---

## Priority 2 — Security Improvements

### Security Awareness Training

Description:
Provide employees with basic cybersecurity training focused on phishing recognition and safe internet practices.

Reason:
Human error is a major cause of cybersecurity incidents.

---

### Enforce Strong Password Policies

Description:
Require employees to use strong, unique passwords and encourage the use of password managers.

Reason:
Strong passwords reduce the likelihood of credential compromise.

---

## Priority 3 — Long-Term Improvements

### Improve Home Network Security Guidance

Description:
Provide employees with guidance on securing their home WiFi networks.

Recommendations may include:

* Use WPA2 or WPA3 encryption
* Update router firmware
* Change default router passwords

---

### Implement Regular Data Backups

Description:
Ensure important company data is regularly backed up and recoverable.

Reason:
Backups reduce the impact of ransomware attacks or accidental data loss.

---

## Summary

By implementing these recommendations, GreenLeaf Accounting LLC can significantly reduce its cybersecurity risk exposure and improve its overall security posture.
