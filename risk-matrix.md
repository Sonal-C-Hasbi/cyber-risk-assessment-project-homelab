# Risk Matrix

A risk matrix helps visualize cybersecurity risks by comparing likelihood and impact.

## Risk Matrix

```text
                IMPACT
           Low     Medium     High
LIKELIHOOD
High       ---     Malware    Phishing
Medium     ---     WiFi Risk  Ransomware
Low        ---     ---        ---
```

## Explanation

High Risk:

* Phishing attacks
* Ransomware

Medium Risk:

* Weak passwords
* Malware infections
* Insecure home WiFi

This matrix helps prioritize which security issues should be addressed first.
