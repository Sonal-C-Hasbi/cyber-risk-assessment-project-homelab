# Risk Scoring Model

## Overview

This document explains the risk scoring methodology used in this cybersecurity risk assessment.

Risk scores are calculated using the following formula:

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

## Likelihood Scale

1 – Rare
2 – Unlikely
3 – Possible
4 – Likely
5 – Very Likely

## Impact Scale

1 – Negligible
2 – Minor
3 – Moderate
4 – Major
5 – Severe

## Risk Levels

1–5 = Low Risk
6–10 = Medium Risk
11–15 = High Risk
16–25 = Critical Risk

## Example

Risk: Phishing Attack

Likelihood: 4
Impact: 5

Risk Score = 4 × 5 = 20

Risk Level: Critical

## Purpose

This scoring model helps prioritize cybersecurity risks and determine which issues should be addressed first.
