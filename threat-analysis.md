# Threat Analysis

## Overview

This document identifies potential cybersecurity threats that could affect GoldLeaf Accounting LLC.

The purpose of this analysis is to understand how attackers might target company systems and data.

These threats will later be used to evaluate cybersecurity risks.

---

## Identified Threats

### Phishing Attacks

Attackers may send fraudulent emails pretending to be legitimate organizations in order to trick employees into revealing credentials or downloading malicious attachments.

Potential Impact:

* Compromise of employee credentials
* Unauthorized access to company systems
* Data theft

Affected Assets:

* Email system
* Employee credentials
* Cloud storage

---

### Ransomware

Malicious software could infect employee laptops through email attachments or malicious downloads.

Potential Impact:

* Encryption of company files
* Business disruption
* Financial loss

Affected Assets:

* Employee laptops
* Cloud file storage
* Accounting software

---

### Weak Passwords or Credential Theft

Employees may use weak passwords or reuse passwords across services.

Potential Impact:

* Unauthorized access to company accounts
* Data exposure

Affected Assets:

* Employee credentials
* Email system
* Cloud services

---

### Insecure Home WiFi Networks

Employees working from home may use poorly secured WiFi networks.

Potential Impact:

* Network interception
* Unauthorized access to company systems

Affected Assets:

* Home WiFi networks
* Employee laptops

---

### Malware Infection

Employees may unknowingly download malicious software from websites or email attachments.

Potential Impact:

* Data theft
* System compromise
* Unauthorized system control

Affected Assets:

* Employee laptops
* Company data
